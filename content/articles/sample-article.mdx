# Understanding Contract Law Basics

Contract law is fundamental to modern business and personal transactions. In this article, we'll explore the key elements that make a contract valid and enforceable.

## What Makes a Contract Valid?

A valid contract requires several essential elements:

1. Offer
2. Acceptance
3. Consideration
4. Intention to create legal relations
5. Capacity to contract

## Common Contract Types

There are several common types of contracts you'll encounter in business:

- Employment contracts
- Service agreements
- Sales contracts
- Lease agreements
